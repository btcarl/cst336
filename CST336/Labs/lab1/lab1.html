Waterfowl inhabit wetlands and marshlands. Wetlands are important for these birds to breed. Logging and devolopment have destroyed many these habitats leading to decrease in the waterfowl population. Due in part to conservation of wetlands and hunting restrictions populations of waterfowl in the United States and Canada is on the rise

Ways to increase Waterfowl Habitat

  • Limit Development in wetland areas
  • Protect marshlands from deforestation
  • Contribute to non profits such as Ducks Unlimited
  • Incentives for turning farmlands back to Wetlands
